DAVector SymbDA::atan ( const DAVector &  da_vector)

Calculate the arctan of the given DA vector. Use formula 4.4.34 and 4.4.42 in the Handbook of Mathematical Functions by Milton Abramowitz and Irene A. Stegun. Assume z0 is the constant part of the given DA vector z and dz = z - z0. In 4.4.34, let z1 = z0 and z2 = dz/(1+z*z0), then the right hand size of 4.4.34 is atan(z). So we get atan(z) = atan(z0) + atan(z2). Use 4.4.42 to calculate atan(z2).

[in]DAvector.
Returns
DA vector.

Call the first group of DA vectors, ivecs, as f, which includes n DA vectors: f_1, f_2, ..., f_n.
